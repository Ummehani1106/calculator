About SRK

Shah Rukh Khan is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality who works in Hindi films. Known as the "Baadshah of Bollywood", he has appeared in more than 80 films and is one of the most successful film stars in the world.

Shah Rukh Khan, often referred to as SRK, is one of the most iconic and influential actors in the history of Indian cinema. Born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India, he started his career in television before making a breakthrough in Bollywood with the film Deewana (1992).

With a career spanning over 30 years, SRK has appeared in over 80 Bollywood films and earned the titles like "King Khan", "Baadshah of Bollywood", and "King of Romance." Known for his charismatic screen presence, powerful dialogue delivery, and versatility, he has a massive fan following across the globe.
